About

Hi, I'm Emily! I'm a Pediatric Occupational Therapist supporting children 1-12 years old out of Manhattan and Brooklyn, New York. I utilize a sensory-based, neuro-affirming, and client-centered approach in therapy.

I graduated with my MA in Occupational Therapy from Boston University in 2018, working as a pediatrics occupational therapist in NYC since. I also co-facilitate Neuro-Lit, a book club that aims to create a supportive and inclusive space for community members of all neurotypes to better understand neurodiversity, challenge assumptions, and expand perspectives. Meaningful occupations to me include reading, painting, hiking, and exploring the many restaurants and museums of NYC.
