About Me

Hi, I'm Emily! I'm a Pediatric Occupational Therapist supporting children 1-12 years old out of Manhattan and Brooklyn, New York. I utilize a sensory-based, neuro-affirming, and client-centered approach in therapy.

I graduated with my M.A. in Occupational Therapy from Boston University in 2018 and have been working as a pediatric occupational therapist in NYC since then. I also co-facilitate NeuroLit, a book club that aims to create a supportive and inclusive space for community members of all neurotypes to better understand neurodiversity, challenge assumptions, and expand perspectives. Meaningful occupations to me include reading, painting, hiking, and exploring the many restaurants and museums of NYC.
