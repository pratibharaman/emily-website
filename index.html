About

Hi, I'm Emily! I'm a Pediatric Occupational Therapist taking a sensory-based, neuro-affirming, and client-centered approach in therapy, supporting children 1-12 years old out of Manhattan and Brooklyn, New York.

What I Offer

  • Private OT sessions, available after 3:30pm in the home, library, or park.
  • Workshops and education sessions on understanding the sensory system, understanding sensory processing differences, and the use of sensory-based strategies in the classroom or the home.

Cash, check, Zelle, and Venmo payments accepted.

Superbills available for you to submit to your insurance company for out-of-network reimbursement upon request.
