What I Offer

  • Occupational Therapy (OT) sessions aimed to support children in their engagement in their daily activities, including but not limited to academic tasks, self-care tasks, and play, with an aim of increasing meaningful activity participation, developing confidence and competence, and greater independence. Sessions may address fine motor skills, visual motor skills, and sensory processing and regulation abilities, activity modifications, environmental modifications, and caregiver education.
  • Comprehensive OT evaluations to determine whether occupational therapy would benefit your child and, if so, to identify appropriate goals and service frequency.
  • Workshops and education sessions on understanding the sensory system and sensory processing abilities, and using sensory-based strategies in the classroom or at home.
